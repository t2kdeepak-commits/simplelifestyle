How to Avoid Distractions and Stay Focused

Distractions are one of the biggest obstacles to productivity and deep work. In our hyper-connected world, interruptions come from countless sources. Learning to minimize and manage distractions is essential for accomplishing meaningful work.

Understanding Distractions

Distractions come in two forms: external interruptions from your environment and internal impulses to shift attention. Both types can be managed with the right strategies and systems.

Manage Digital Distractions

Turn off all non-essential notifications on your phone and computer. Use website blockers during focused work sessions to prevent access to distracting sites.

Set Boundaries With Others

Communicate your need for uninterrupted time to colleagues, family, and roommates.

Manage Internal Distractions

When random thoughts pop up, quickly write them down and return to your task.

Create Distraction-Free Zones

Designate specific spaces for focused work where distracting items are not present.
